# PaintBot Prime Strategy

## 1. Start of Match
- **Spread Out:** At the start of the match, the three tanks should spread out to different sections of the map's edges. One targets the top wall, one the left wall, and one the bottom wall.

## 2. Movement Route
- **Patrol Edges:** Once at the walls, tanks stay close to the edge and continuously move **back and forth** along the walls in a predictable patrol route.
- **Top Speed:** Movement priority is high. Always maintain top speed while patrolling.

## 3. Dealing with Power Ups
- **Opportunistic Collection:** If a power power-up (green plus) spawns close to the patrol path, divert slightly to pick it up.
- **Ignore Far Powerups:** Do not make large detours or cross the center of the map to get a power-up. Keep to the assigned patrol edge.

## 4. Turret Scanning (Sweep & Lock)
- **See-Saw Sweep:** The turret should continuously sweep back and forth in a "see-saw" motion to scan for targets.
- **Lock & Predict:** When an enemy tank (blue square) is detected, the turret must stop sweeping, lock onto the target, and aim ahead of it to predict its movement. Avoid losing lock.

## 5. Firing Rules
- **Direct Line of Sight:** Only fire if the enemy is directly in the firing line and within clear range. 
- **Don't Waste Shots:** Do not fire if sweeping and the enemy is just outside the direct path or barely clipped by the scanner.
- **Collateral Shots:** It is acceptable (or encouraged) to fire if a power-up or an enemy behind a power-up is in the direct line of fire.
- **Priority 1:** Firing has the absolute highest priority ("FIRE! FIRE! FIRE! FIRE!").

## 6. Priorities Summary
1. **Fire! Fire! Fire!** (Shoot when locked on)
2. **Top Speed** (Maintain high speed on the patrol route)
3. **Sweep, Follow + Predict** (Continuous scanning, lock onto enemies, predict movement)
